MoodDrake Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Data Controller

As the MoodDrake mobile application, we are committed to ensuring the security of your personal data and protecting your privacy in accordance with all applicable data protection laws.

Data Controller: MoodDrake

2. Collected Personal Data

The following types of data are processed within the scope of the application:

  • Account information: email address, password (stored securely with hashing)
  • Device information: operating system, device model, IP address, language, and country information
  • Usage data: session duration, number of interactions, in-app navigation statistics
  • Advertising data: ad display and click history, advertising ID (AAID / IDFA)
  • Analytics data: anonymized performance and usage statistics

3. Purposes and Legal Basis for Data Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Creating an account, logging in, and ensuring secure access
  • Monitoring app performance and improving user experience
  • Displaying and personalizing advertisements

MoodDrake is a mobile application that operates on an ad-supported and personalized content model. Displaying advertisements and personalized content is an integral part of the service.

By using the application, you consent to the processing of your personal data for advertising and analytics purposes. This processing is necessary to provide the app free of charge.

If you do not wish your data to be processed for advertising purposes, you can delete your account or stop using the application.

Legal Basis:

  • GDPR Article 6(1)(b): Performance of a contract (operation of the app and maintenance of the ad-supported structure)
  • GDPR Article 6(1)(a): User's explicit consent (for personalized ads)
  • Turkish KVKK Article 5: Explicit consent and data processing directly related to the establishment or performance of a contract

4. Data Sharing

MoodDrake does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Your data may only be shared in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations, upon request from authorized public authorities
  • With infrastructure service providers that support the technical operation of the app (such as hosting or email providers)
  • With third-party advertising and analytics providers (such as Google AdMob, Supabase, Meta Audience Network, etc.) solely for performance tracking and personalized ad delivery

All data transfers are carried out with the necessary security measures under KVKK and GDPR.

5. Data Retention Period

Your data is retained as long as your account remains active.

When you delete your account, all your personal data (including backup systems) is permanently deleted or anonymized within 30 days.

6. Security Measures

Necessary technical and administrative measures are taken to ensure the security of your personal data:

  • Passwords are stored using hash algorithms and cannot be accessed in plain text
  • All data transmissions are encrypted via SSL/TLS protocol
  • Access is restricted only to authorized personnel

7. User Rights

Users have the following rights under applicable data protection laws:

  • Access their data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete their data

All these rights can be exercised via the in-app "Account Settings" menu or by email.

Users who wish to stop data processing must delete their accounts.

8. Cookies and Analytics

MoodDrake may use cookies or similar technologies for the purpose of displaying advertisements, measuring performance, and improving user experience.

This data does not allow direct identification of your identity; advertising identifiers are processed in an anonymized manner.

9. Disclaimer

The content of the application is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical, psychological, or legal advice.

MoodDrake is not responsible for interruptions, data loss, or security errors caused by third-party service providers.

TaskNest Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

TaskNest is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from users.

1. No Data Collection

TaskNest does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage data
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers

2. Local Device Storage Only

All tasks, notes, and reminders you create inside TaskNest are stored ONLY on your device. Your data:

  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Is never transmitted outside your device
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app

3. Third-Party Services

TaskNest does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-processing services.

4. Children's Privacy

Since no personal data is collected, TaskNest is safe for users of all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the latest version will always be accessible within the app.
